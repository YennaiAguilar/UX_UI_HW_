SLAM is a non-profit based in Utah that provides after-school lessons, performance, composition,
songwriting, recording, and summer camp programs for K-12 kids of all backgrounds, and skill levels. SLAM was founded in response to an unforeseen need for financial aid among families enrolled in musicgarage.org, the awardwinning after-school, rock music performance program created and managed by SLAM Executive Director and CoFounder, Steve Auerbach from 2009-2019.

The problem

Working parents who want to support their children’s musical interests and development don’t have the budget to spend on classes or musical instruments for their kids. SLAM offers free options for their programs, but the website is cluttered which makes it difficult to make an informed decision.

The solution

We aimed to create a seamless user journey that highlights the key information about Slam's offerings, such as its educational approach, affordability, and the positive impact it can have on children's musical development. This guided us in creating an engaging and informative website that resonates with parents' needs and ultimately encourages them to explore Slam further.
